A Little Bit About Me

I graduated High School, in Arkansas, in 2000. I also went to school in the greater Chicago, Il area. I joined the military in 2000, the day after I graduated high school, and I got out in 2004.

I've gone to college off an on, finally earning my Associates Degree in Applied Science (emphasis on I.T.). I got married to my lovely wife, Ashley, in Feb of 2016. We have each have a boy and now we have a girl on the way (Jan 2018).

I work for a fortune 500 company by day and I toil away by (some) nights on Papio Web Design, growing the business, and growing awareness of flawless function and beautiful form.

Currently pursuing my Bachelor's of Information Technology: Web Application Development and I'm nearly complete.
